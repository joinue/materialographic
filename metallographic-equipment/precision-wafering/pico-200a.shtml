PICO-200A Automatic Table Feed Saw

The PICO-200A Automatic Table Feed Saw offers micrometer-level accuracy for sectioning a variety of materials. With an 8-inch blade, variable speed (500-3000 rpm), and a 2-inch cutting capacity, it ensures precise, efficient performance. Its built-in laser alignment ensures precise sample positioning, optimizing cutting accuracy.

Features

    • High Cutting Capacity: Supports up to 2-inch (50 mm) cutting capacity
    • Efficient Specimen Feed: Features a table feed for smooth operation
    • Laser Alignment: enables precise sample positioning for enhanced cutting accuracy
    • Variable Speed Control: Continuously adjustable speed (500-3000 rpm)
    • Integrated Coolant System: 5-liter coolant tank for effective heat management
