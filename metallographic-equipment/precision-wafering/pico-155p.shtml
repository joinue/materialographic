PICO-155P Precision Wafering Saw

The PICO-155P Precision Wafering Saw is built for precise metallographic sectioning, featuring analog controls for intuitive use. With a 2-inch cutting capacity, dual nozzle coolant system, and variable speed (200-1500 rpm) with LED display, it adapts seamlessly to different materials. Its 2-micron micrometer feed accuracy ensures exceptional precision, making it perfect for labs that demand reliable, accurate performance.

Features

  • Intuitive Controls: Analog controls for precise manual operation
  • Robust Construction: Built for reliable performance in demanding environments
  • Efficient Cooling: Dual nozzle coolant system for optimal cutting conditions
  • Variable Speed: Adjustable speed (200-1500 rpm) with LED display for adaptability
  • Micrometer Precision: Accuracy of 2 microns for precise sectioning
