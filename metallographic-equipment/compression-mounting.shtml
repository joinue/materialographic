Compression Mounting Presses

Compression mounting presses are vital for metallographic sample preparation, encapsulating specimens in resin molds to protect surfaces for analysis. Using hydraulic or pneumatic systems, they apply consistent pressure and heat to produce high-quality mounts. Essential in industries like aerospace and biomedical, they ensure precise sample preparation for accurate analysis.

TP-7500S - Hydraulic Compression Mounting Press

The TP-7500S is a robust hydraulic compression mounting press engineered for high-performance metallographic sample preparation. Its hydraulic pressure system ensures consistent and precise mounting results, making it ideal for demanding laboratory applications.

Recirculating Coolant Tank

Recirculating tanks improve compression mounting press performance by maintaining optimal temperatures and reducing water usage through efficient coolant circulation. This ensures effective heat dissipation, extends equipment lifespan, and supports sustainable operations. Ideal for labs seeking greater efficiency and a smaller environmental footprint.

TP-TANK - Recirculating Coolant System

The TP-TANK is an environmentally efficient recirculating coolant tank designed to minimize water usage for cooling TERAPRESS compression mounting presses. This self-contained system ensures optimal cooling while significantly reducing water requirements.

  • Pump: 24V DC Diaphragm Pump, 1.3 GPM (5 L/min)
  • Volume of Tank: 14 gallons (53 L)
  • Electrical Specifications: 110V or 220V 50/60 Hz
  • Dimensions (WxDxH): 800 x 450 x 495 mm (31.5 x 16.5 x 19.5 inches)
  • Weight: 18 kg (40 lbs)

With its robust design, the TP-TANK efficiently supports the cooling needs of TERAPRESS presses, offering a sustainable solution for maintaining optimal operating conditions.
