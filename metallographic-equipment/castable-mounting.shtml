Vacuum & Pressure Mounting

Vacuum and pressure mounting are key in metallography for embedding specimens in resin. Vacuum mounting removes air bubbles for complete resin penetration, while pressure mounting enhances infiltration and minimizes defects. Combined, they produce high-quality mounts with clear surfaces, crucial for accurate analysis in industries like aerospace and materials science.

UV Curing System

UV curing rapidly solidifies polymers using ultraviolet light, making it ideal for hardening adhesives, coatings, and resins in metallography. This process reduces processing time, lowers energy use, and minimizes thermal stress on samples. With precise control over curing conditions, it ensures consistent, high-quality results, benefiting industries like electronics and biomedical manufacturing.
