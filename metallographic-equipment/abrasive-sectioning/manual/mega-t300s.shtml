MEGA-T300S Manual Abrasive Cutter

The MEGA-T300S Abrasive Cutter by PACE Technologies offers precise manual cutting with a 12-inch blade and powerful servo motor. Its manual controls allow fine adjustments for detailed sample preparation. A T-slotted table and adjustable cutting forces ensure stability and accuracy.
