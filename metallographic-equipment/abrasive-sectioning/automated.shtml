MEGA-T300A

The MEGA-T300A Automated Cutter combines precision and automation for efficient cutting applications. It features both automated and manual table feeds with a manual wheel feed, offering flexibility and control in various operations.

  • Maximum Cut-Off Blade Diameter: 300 mm (~12 inches)
  • Cutting Capacity:
    • Diameter: Up to 4 inches (100 mm)
    • Height x Depth: 3 x 8 inches (75 x 200 mm)
  • Z-Axis Wheel Feed Range: 3.9 inches (100 mm)
  • Y-Axis Table Feed: 7 inches (175 mm) with a feed rate of 0.04-0.20 inches/sec (0.1 - 5 mm/sec)
  • X-Axis Table Feed: 1.8 inches (45 mm) with a feed rate of 0.04-0.40 inches/sec (0.1 - 10 mm/sec)
  • Pulse Mode: 0.5-5A

The t-slotted stainless steel table, measuring 11 x 18.25 inches (280 x 470 mm), provides a stable platform for accurate cuts. The MEGA-T300A's automated and manual capabilities ensure precise performance in demanding applications, making it ideal for high-precision tasks.
