MEGA-T300A Automated Abrasive Cutter

The MEGA-T300A Abrasive Saw offers flexible cutting with automatic and manual modes. Its 12-inch blade and 15 kW servo motor power high-efficiency operations. Automatic Y and optional X-axis feeds ensure precise, rapid cuts—perfect for industrial material analysis.
