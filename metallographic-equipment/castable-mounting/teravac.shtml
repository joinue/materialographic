Metallographic Castable Vacuum Impregnation Chambers

Castable Vacuum Impregnation Chambers

The TeraVAC and TeraVAC Pro vacuum impregnation systems are designed to improve resin penetration in samples with voids and porosity. The TeraVAC offers a simple, economical solution with vacuum pouring, room-pressure curing, and a tilting, retractable pouring arm for ease of use and reliable results. The TeraVAC Pro builds on this capability with programmable, automated control of vacuum and cycle parameters, a larger chamber capacity, an integrated rotating table for efficient mold positioning, and a user-friendly, low-noise design suited for higher-throughput laboratory environments.
