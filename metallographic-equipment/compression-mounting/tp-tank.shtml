TP-TANK Recirculating Coolant System

The TP-TANK Recirculating Coolant System optimizes cooling for compression mounting presses like the TERAPRESS series. With a 14-gallon (53-liter) capacity and 24V DC pump delivering 1.3 GPM, it ensures consistent cooling and reduced water usage. It is ideal for labs focused on sustainable and efficient sample preparation.

Features

  • Efficient Cooling: 14-gallon (53-liter) capacity ensures consistent cooling for prolonged use.
  • Reduced Water Usage: Recirculating design minimizes water consumption, promoting sustainability.
  • Powerful Pump: 24V DC diaphragm pump delivers 1.3 GPM (5 L/min) for optimal coolant flow.
  • Compact and Durable: Designed for easy integration with TERAPRESS series mounting presses.
  • Enhanced Operational Efficiency: Maintains consistent temperatures during intensive mounting operations.
