Hydraulic Compression Mounting - TP-7500S

TP-7500S Hydraulic Compression Mounting Press

The TP-7500S Hydraulic Compression Mounting Press ensures precise, consistent sample preparation with hydraulic pressure. It supports 1 to 2-inch mounts and features a 1600-watt heater for heating up to 392°F (200°C). Its water-cooling system with manual or automated controls makes it ideal for labs needing reliable, repeatable results.

Features

  • Powerful Force Application: Uses hydraulic pressure for consistent and reliable mounting.
  • Versatile Mount Sizes: Supports 1 to 2-inch (25-50 mm) molds with fixed and interchangeable cylinders.
  • Robust Heating System: Features a 1600-watt heater with 110V or 220V options.
  • Precise Thermal Control: Offers temperature management up to 392°F (200°C) with water-cooling options.
  • Heavy-Duty Design: Handles incoming pressures up to 6460 psi for demanding applications.
