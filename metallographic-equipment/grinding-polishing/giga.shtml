Vibratory Polisher - GIGA-S

GIGA-S Vibratory Polisher

The GIGA-S Vibratory Polisher by PACE Technologies ensures superior surface finishes and eliminates subsurface damage, perfect for EBSD (Electron Backscatter Diffraction) sample preparation. With a PZT motor for precise vibration control, a leak-free two-part bowl, and interchangeable 9" or 12" polishing bowls, it offers versatile, worry-free polishing with exceptional flatness and planarity.
