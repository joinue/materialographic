ZETA-2000S Automated Abrasive Dispenser

The ZETA-2000S is an advanced automated abrasive dispenser designed to enhance the precision and efficiency of your metallographic sample preparation process. Equipped with programmable peristaltic pumps, it allows for exact metering of abrasives and lubricants, ensuring consistent and reproducible polishing results. The intuitive touchscreen interface offers easy control over dispensing parameters, making the ZETA-2000S an essential tool for laboratories that demand accuracy and repeatability.
