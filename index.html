About PACE Technologies

Founded by Dr. Donald and Cathy Zipperian, PACE Technologies has supported metallographic sample preparation and microstructural analysis for over 25 years. As a family-owned company, we focus on providing reliable equipment and consumables to help laboratories and researchers achieve consistent, high-quality results.

We are committed to offering practical solutions, personal support, and the experience needed to meet the evolving challenges of metallography and materials testing.
